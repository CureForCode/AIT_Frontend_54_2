How to use:

  1. Press the “Get Cat Fact” button to generate a new random cat fact.
  2. Press the button again to load more facts. All previous facts will be kept!
  3. You can have up to 10 fact cards at a time.
  4. Once you reach 10 cards, pressing the button will clear all cards and start over.
  5. Enjoy and discover something new!
